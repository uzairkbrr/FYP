VOICE-DRIVEN STUDENT SUPPORT IN URDU & ENGLISH

1
Introduction

Every admission season, the FAST-NUCES Peshawar front desk handles thousands of repetitive calls about admissions, fees, scholarships, and programs. MahirConnect is an Urdu-first voice assistant that answers these queries instantly in fluent Roman-Urdu or English, around the clock — reducing front-desk workload while giving students reliable information in their native language.

2
Project Objectives
  • Provide instant, accurate Urdu and English voice responses to common student queries.
  • Reduce front-desk call volume during peak admission season.
  • Make official information accessible 24/7 in the student's preferred language.
  • Support both voice and text through a single conversational interface.
  • Stay strictly on-topic — politely decline unrelated requests.
