Admission Fee Rs 30,000 admission ke waqt sirf aik martaba ada ki jati hai. Agar is University ka koi graduate kisi higher degree program mein admission le, toh admission fee dobara ada nahi karni parti.

Admission ke waqt Rs 20,000 ka refundable security deposit bhi ada karna hota hai, jo degree mukammal hone par ya admission band karne par wapas mil jata hai.

Tuition Fee har semester shuru hone se pehle mukammal tor par ada karni hoti hai aur yeh fee har saal nazar-e-saani (review) ke tabe hai. Fall 2025 semester se laagu academic year 2025-26 ke liye fi credit hour tuition fee Rs. 11,000 hai, jo BBA, BS, MBA, MS aur PhD tamam programs par yaksan laagu hoti hai.

Fi semester ki kul fee ka hisaab lagane ka tareeqa: us semester ke credit hours ko Rs. 11,000 se multiply karein aur uss mein Rs. 2,500 (Student Activities Fund) add karein. Aam tor par aik semester mein 15 se 18 credit hours hote hain, isliye aam semester fee Rs. 167,500 (15 credit hours ke saath) se le kar Rs. 200,500 (18 credit hours ke saath) tak hoti hai.

Agar koi shakhs bilaa program aur semester specify kiye sirf "fee kya hai?" poochhe, toh aam standard load 15 credit hours maan kar jawab diya jata hai: 15 x Rs. 11,000 = Rs. 165,000 tuition + Rs. 2,500 Student Activities Fund = Rs. 167,500 fi semester.

Co-curricular aur extra-curricular activities ko support karne ke liye aik Student Activities Fund qayam kiya gaya hai. Students is fund mein fi semester Rs 2,500 jama karwate hain (Fall 2024 semester se laagu).

Miscellaneous fees mein darj zail charges shamil hain: Additional Transcript Fee Rs. 300, Campus Transfer Fee Rs. 5,000, Temporary Campus Transfer Fee Rs. 2,000, Degree Correction Fee Rs. 2,000, Semester Freeze Fee Rs. 2,000 (classes shuru hone ke do hafton ke andar), Late Registration Fee / Freeze Fee / Course Withdrawal Rs. 5,000 (classes shuru hone ke doosre hafte ke baad), Retake Sessional Exam Fee Rs. 2,000 fi course, Retake Final Exam Fee Rs. 2,000 fi course ya lab, Paper Rechecking Fee Rs. 500 fi course ya lab, Issuance of Certificate ya Letter Fee Rs. 300, aur Applicant Admission Fee Rs. 3,000. Yeh tamam fees non-refundable hain.

Late payment fine poori outstanding fee (maujooda aur arrears dono) par lagayi jati hai aur har 1,000 rupees par roznaa Re 1.00 ke hisaab se charge hoti hai. Fee ki due date ke do mahine baad registration suspend kar di jati hai.

Fee refund policy ke mutabiq Admission Fee non-refundable hai, Security Deposit degree mukammal hone par ya admission band karne par wapas mil jata hai, aur Tuition Fee Higher Education Commission (HEC) ki nazar-saani shuda guidelines ke mutabiq refund ki jati hai jo 20-Dec-2024 (HEC Letter Issuance Date) se laagu hain.

Tuition fee refund ka schedule yeh hai: classes shuru hone ke 10ve din tak 100% refund, 15ve din tak 80% refund, 20ve din tak 60% refund, 30ve din tak 50% refund, aur 31ve din ke baad koi refund nahi milta. Dinon ki ginti mein working days aur chutiyan dono shamil ki jati hain.

Natija (result) announce hone ke baad, agar koi student admission ki zaroori eligibility requirement poori nahi karta toh uska admission cancel ho jata hai. Aise case mein fee refund uske result announce hone ki tareekh se calculate kiya jata hai.

Fee ada karne ke do tareeqe hain: pehla yeh ke challan form print kar ke kisi bhi qareebi Faysal Bank branch mein cash deposit karwayein; doosra Kuickpay payment gateway ke zariye bank account se ada karein. Kuickpay ke liye Internet Banking, Mobile Banking, ya ATM mein sign in karein, Bill Payment section mein ja kar 'kuickpay' select karein, voucher ya invoice number (Institution ID prefix ke saath) darj karein, aur payment confirm kar dein.

PhD thesis par research ka amal tab shuru hota hai jab Comprehensive Examination pass ho jaye aur Graduate Studies Committee se synopsis manzoor ho jaye. PhD thesis submit karne ke liye kam az kam aik research paper HEC approved category journal mein publish hona zaroori hai. Thesis original research par mabni honi chahiye aur submission se pehle plagiarism test se guzarni chahiye jo foreign experts ko bheji jati hai. Thesis ki evaluation mein kam az kam do foreign experts shamil hote hain; agar woh acceptance ki sifarish karte hain to local committee jo do local aur aik internal FAST-NUCES expert par mushtamil hoti hai, thesis ko review karti hai. Local evaluation ke baad candidate ko local committee ke samne thesis defend karni hoti hai aur unke approval par successful qarar diya jata hai.

National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences aik PhD program offer karta hai jo duniya ke behtareen professionals tayar karne ke liye design kiya gaya hai jo zimmedar shehri hon. Program ko mutanawwe backgrounds se promising students attract karne aur behtareen taleem dene par zor diya jata hai baghair kisi maali, nasal, jins, ya mazhabi farq ke. PhD program mein 18 credit hours coursework aur 24 credit hours research work zaroori hai, jo total 42 credit hours banta hai, aur maximum duration chay saal hai jo genuine wajah se aath saal tak barhaya ja sakta hai. Admission criteria mein 18 saal taleem ke saath MS/MPhil/MBA degree HEC se manzoor shuda university se honi chahiye, minimum CGPA 3.00 ya first division, aur GRE/GAT ya FAST-NUCES admission test mein kam az kam 60% score hone chahiye, jisme borderline cases ke liye zero semester ke zariye provisional admission ki sahulat bhi mojood hai. Graduate Studies Committee (GSC) jo Board of Advanced Studies and Research (BASR) ke zariye tait hoti hai, admissions, progress reviews, aur policy recommendations ko monitor karti hai, Supervisors aur zarurat par Co-Supervisors appoint karti hai mutaliqa guidelines ke tehat. PhD coursework mein minimum 3.00 CGPA aur laazmi attendance required hai, aur har semester mein zyada se zyada teen courses karna allowed hai, jab ke dusri universities se limited courses ki ijazat bhi hai approval ke sath. Comprehensive examination monolithic hai jo PhD courses cover karta hai, saal mein do martaba hota hai, aur maximum do attempts allowed hain pass hone ke liye. Research phase mein thesis research registration kam az kam chaar semesters ke liye zaroori hai, jisme students ko satisfactory grades hasil karne hote hain, aur reputable abroad institutes mein research attachments bhi kiye ja sakte hain jo cumulatively barah mahine se zyada na hon. Thesis submission required research aur kam az kam aik HEC-manzoor shuda "W" ya "X" category journal mein research paper publication ke baad hota hai; thesis original honi chahiye, English mein likhi jani chahiye, similarity index 19% se kam hona chahiye, HEC aur University ki policies ke mutabiq plagiarism aur generative AI par amal daramad honi chahiye, aur kam az kam do foreign reviewers se evaluation zaroori hai. Final thesis defense public event hoti hai jo teen experts ki examination committee ke samne hoti hai jisme Supervisor bhi shamil hota hai, aur decisions mein PhD award karna shamil ho sakta hai minor changes ke sath ya baghair. Admission forgery, disciplinary infractions, poor progress, ya dues na dene par band ya cancel kiya ja sakta hai, semester freezing aur extension applications ke liye sakht rules hain. PhD registration ka transfer FAST-NUCES aur dusri universities ke darmiyan, aur supervisor changes intra-aur inter-university basis par committees ki manzoori aur HEC guidelines ke mutabiq manage hotay hain. Supervision withdrawal formal warnings ke baad hoti hai aur GSC ke intervention ka sabab ban sakti hai. Fees ki payment admission par mukammal hoti hai jo university prospectus mein tafseeli hai. Ethical guidelines mein merit ke bunyad par authorship, duplicate publication se parhez, munasib acknowledgments, aur plagiarism, fabrication, aur falsification ki zero tolerance shamil hai. University PhD scholars ke liye fellowship program bhi deti hai jisme full tuition waivers aur monthly stipend diya jata hai jo academic milestones jaise coursework completion, comprehensive examination, publication requirements, aur full-time student status maintain karne par munhasir hai. PhD thesis detailed formatting guidelines ke mutabiq honi chahiye jo manuscript preparation, pagination, chapter structure, referencing style, aur binding university ke green environment obligations ke mutabiq define karta hai. Supervisors expert guidance, training, regular meetings, performance feedback, research integrity ka khayal, professional development ke mauqay aur thesis submission aur defense ke liye students ko prepare karte hain. Co-supervisors cross-disciplinary research conditions ya khas halaat mein appoint kiye jate hain jinke liye approvals aur remuneration guidelines tay hain. Thesis submission ke liye zaroori documents mein admission confirmation, transcripts, comprehensive exam results, plagiarism report, journal publication aur correspondence, aur foreign reviewer consents shamil hain. PhD synopsis mein introduction, literature review, methodology, tafseeli work plan, expected conclusions, aur references shamil hone chahiye jo prescribed format mein present kiye jate hain. Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) Mapping Table dissertation mein shamil honi chahiye jisey supervisor validate karta hai. Ethical research compliance ko dissertation mein certify karna zaroori hai, aur agar generative AI use hota hai to iska wazeh izhar bhi hona chahiye traditional authorship aur integrity statements ke sath.
